[prefix{l}]The Edit Sign piece [prefix{}]
This will set all Signs in the area to the text specified by the text pieces connected. This could be either Vanilla Signs or [link{pneumaticcraft:block/aphorismTile}]Aphorism Tiles[link{}].

Every connected Text piece represents one line. By connecting multiple text pieces you can set a multi-line message.
